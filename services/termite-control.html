Signs of Termite Infestation

Mud Tubes

Pencil-sized tunnels on foundation walls or wooden structures

Hollow Wood

Wood that sounds hollow when tapped

Discarded Wings

Piles of wings near windows or doors

Frass (Droppings)

Small piles of wood-colored pellets

Our Treatment Methods

A continuous chemical barrier is created in the soil around your foundation. This is the most common and effective method for immediate elimination and long-term prevention.

  • Immediate protection upon application
  • Prevents termites from entering the structure
  • Typically lasts 5-10 years
  • EPA-approved, low-impact products available

Strategically placed bait stations around your property attract foraging termites, who then carry the slow-acting toxin back to the colony, eliminating it completely.

  • Eliminates entire colonies, including the queen
  • Environmentally friendly with minimal chemical use
  • Ongoing monitoring and maintenance
  • Ideal for sensitive environments

Direct application of borate-based products to exposed wood during construction or renovation provides long-lasting protection against termites and other wood-destroying organisms.

  • Lifetime protection for treated wood
  • Prevents future infestations
  • Also effective against carpenter ants and beetles
  • Best for new construction or renovations
