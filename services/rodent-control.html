Don't let rats and mice take over your property. Our comprehensive rodent control program eliminates existing infestations and seals entry points to prevent their return.

Complete Rodent Elimination & Prevention

Rodents pose serious health risks and can cause significant property damage by chewing through wires, insulation, and structural materials. Our multi-step approach ensures complete elimination and long-term protection.

Thorough Inspection

Complete property assessment to identify entry points, nesting areas, and the extent of infestation.

Elimination

Strategic trapping and baiting programs tailored to your specific rodent species and infestation level.

Exclusion

Seal entry points and install barriers to prevent rodents from re-entering your property.

Monitoring

Ongoing monitoring and maintenance to ensure your property remains rodent-free.

Signs of Rodent Infestation

Droppings

Small, dark pellets found in cabinets, pantries, or along baseboards

Gnaw Marks

Chewed wires, furniture, food packaging, or structural wood

Noises

Scratching, squeaking, or scurrying sounds in walls or ceilings

Nests

Shredded paper, fabric, or insulation in hidden areas

Our Rodent Control Process

Our certified technician conducts a comprehensive inspection of your property to:

  • Identify rodent species (rats vs. mice)
  • Locate entry points and nesting areas
  • Assess the extent of infestation
  • Evaluate property conditions contributing to the problem
  • Document damage and contamination

Based on inspection findings, we implement a customized treatment plan:

  • Strategic placement of tamper-resistant bait stations
  • Trapping programs for immediate reduction
  • Exterior baiting for perimeter control
  • Attic and crawl space treatments
  • Safe, EPA-approved rodenticides

Preventing re-entry is crucial for long-term success:

  • Seal gaps and cracks larger than 1/4 inch
  • Install door sweeps and weather stripping
  • Screen vents and chimney openings
  • Repair damaged soffits and fascia
  • Install rodent-proof barriers

We don't just treat and leave. Our service includes:

  • Follow-up inspections to verify elimination
  • Monitoring stations for early detection
  • Recommendations for sanitation improvements
  • Ongoing maintenance programs available
  • Guaranteed results with our warranty

Health Risks of Rodents

Rodents can transmit serious diseases and trigger allergies. Don't delay treatment if you suspect an infestation.

  • Hantavirus
  • Salmonellosis
  • Leptospirosis
  • Fleas & ticks
  • Fire hazards from chewed wires
  • Allergic reactions
