Application Methods

Advanced technology that applies an electric charge to disinfectant droplets, allowing them to wrap around surfaces and coat areas that traditional spraying misses.

Best for:
  • Large commercial spaces
  • Complex surfaces and equipment
  • Hard-to-reach areas
  • Quick application with complete coverage

Ultra-low volume fogging creates a fine mist that penetrates deep into cracks, crevices, and porous surfaces. Ideal for comprehensive space treatment.

Best for:
  • Whole-room disinfection
  • HVAC system treatment
  • Areas with confirmed contamination
  • Post-construction cleaning

Hand-application of disinfectant to high-touch surfaces ensuring proper dwell time for maximum effectiveness.

Best for:
  • High-touch surfaces (doorknobs, light switches)
  • Electronics and sensitive equipment
  • Food preparation areas
  • Detailed, precision cleaning

For maximum protection, we often combine multiple methods to ensure complete coverage and lasting protection.

  • Fogging for air and surface treatment
  • Electrostatic spray for comprehensive coverage
  • Manual wiping for high-touch points
  • Protective coating application for lasting protection (optional)
